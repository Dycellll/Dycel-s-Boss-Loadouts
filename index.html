DYCEL'S BOSS LOADOUTS

ABOUT THE MOD

Dycel's Boss Loadouts is a Terraria mod built for seamless boss preparation. It allows you to save, organize, and instantly load gear setups for any boss. Includes full inventory handling, armor, accessories, ammo, mounts, hooks, defeated bosses, and even Calamity-specific permanent buffs. It also includes tabs to toggle defeated bosses, permanent buffs, and more.

  • ✅ Create as many loadouts as you'd like
  • ✅ Folder system for organization
  • ✅ Share your loadouts with files
  • ✅ Toggle permanent bosses/buffs
  • ✅ Calamity Mod support
